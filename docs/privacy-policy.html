Privacy Policy for Lunga

Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lunga. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our civic engagement platform, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address (e.g., when you register or contact us).
  • User Content: Reports, complaints, comments, and issues you post on the platform.
  • Location Data: To help tag and locate issues you report.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our app.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform.
  • Process and display the civic issues you report.
  • Communicate with you, including for customer service and updates.
  • Improve our application and user experience.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our application.
  • Law enforcement or other authorities if required by law.
  • Other users of the platform (only public content like your reports and comments).

5. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@lunga.app
